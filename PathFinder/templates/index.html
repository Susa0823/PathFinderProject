PathFinder is an interactive search engine that receives search queries and prompts from its users,it can provide resources citing the responses it generated and saves these resources as well as the type of questions the user asked as part of the users interests.Part of our aim at PathFinder is to provide a simplified way for users to explore a range of topics they are interested in.

Ask A.I

How does this work?

Sign-up

  • Head over to the 'Ask A.I' Page and ask a question to Charlie on a topic of interest.

  • Charlie will answer your questions in the simplest manner possible making it easy for you to understand.

  • Along with answers Charlie scours the web to find you the best and most helpful resources pertaining to your question so you do not have to.

  • Explore and Save your favourite resources to view them later in the 'Resources List'
