GDPR Policy for Pathfinder

Categories of Personal Information Gathered

We gather personal information that is required for the delivery of our services. This comprises of:

  • Users' entered search terms and prompts
  • Materials made available as a result of user inquiries
  • User preferences determined on search history and recently saved resources

Processing of Data

To give users access to an interactive search engine that enables them to explore a variety of topics of interest, we process users' personal data. Additionally, we use personal data to enhance our offerings and cater users' home pages to their preferences and past searches.

How the Data is Collected

We collect data through users' interaction with our search engine and saved resources.

Legal Justification for Data Processing

User consent serves as our legal justification for processing personal data. While using our services, users have the choice to give their consent for the collection, handling, storage, and sharing of their personal data.

Data Deletion and Retention

We only keep user data as long as it's essential to deliver our services and as long as it's mandated by law. When the data is no longer required for these purposes, we will securely destroy it in line with GDPR regulations.

Sharing of Personal Information

In order to help us deliver our services, we may share personal information with outside service providers. We make sure that these service providers adhere to the GDPR and have adequate security measures in place to protect personal information.

Data Protection Rights

Data subjects' rights include the ability to view, update, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of their personal information. Also, they are entitled to data portability and the capacity to complain to a supervisory authority. Access limits, data encryption, and regular backups are just a few of the security precautions we've put in place to safeguard the safety of your personal information.

Marketing

We do not use personal information for marketing purposes.

Cookies

Cookies are small data files stored on users' devices when they access our search engine.

How Cookies are Used

We use cookies to personalize users' experience, remember preferences, and analyze website traffic.

Types of Cookies Used

We use functional cookies, which are necessary for the search engine to function, and analytics cookies, which help us understand how users interact with our search engine.

Manage Cookies

Users can manage their cookies through their browser settings.

Privacy Policies of Other Websites

Our privacy policy applies only to our interactive search engine. Users should read the privacy policies of other websites they access through our search engine.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our website.
